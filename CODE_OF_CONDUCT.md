# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment,  
we as members of the hive pledge to make participation in Buzzword
a harassment-free experience for everyone.

Mostly.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:
- Act in good faith
- Respect the hive
- Accept the hive’s decisions without complaint
- Understand that disagreement does not imply wrongdoing

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Attempting to exploit the system
- Arguing with the hive
- Insisting something is a bug when it is clearly a beeture
- Repeatedly asking “but why?” after a decision has been made

## Enforcement

All violations will be reviewed by the hive.

The hive’s decisions are final.
Appeals will not be considered.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces,
and also applies when an individual is attempting to explain
why their idea was “obviously better.”

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common sense.
